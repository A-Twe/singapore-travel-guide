Our Top Recommendations

Hawker Centres

Singapore is famous for it's love of food which is shown by the 114 hawkers centres throughout Singapore. This local delight is something you don't want to miss!

Sentosa

Sentosa is actually a man-made island built by Singapore! With everything from Universial Studios to Casino's, Sentosa has something for everyone

Gardens by the Bay

Gardens by the bay is a key aspect to Singapore's "City in a Garden" vision. Located in central Singapore, this is a great place for a day trip and easily accessible
