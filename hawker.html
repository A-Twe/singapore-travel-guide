Hawker Centers

Hawker Centers

Where can travelers taste good local food at resonable prices in Singapore? The answer is simple - hawker centres (also known as food courts). Get a taste of Singapore with a wide variety of local dishes ranging from Chicken Rice to Chilli Crab and Bak Kut Teh. There are hundreds of food courts and thousands of food stalls in Singapore so there's something for everyone!

Cheap

Around $3-$5 per meal

Wide Variety

Over 3,000 food stalls

Top Rated

Very popular destination

Key information

Arriving

Before you embark on your Hawker food adventure, be sure to save yourseslf a seat by placing a packet of tissues on the table. This let's other people know that you have reserved the table whilst you are going to get your food. Some Hawkers sell napkins at the entrance, however we recommend bringing your own.


Food Hunting

Now the fun part, selecting your food! There are plenty of options to choose from so you won't be struggling for choice. Typically, food stalls will number their dishes so take note of the dish number and let the cashier know. Unless the stall specifies, most hawkers are self-service which means you need to stand and wait for your food.*Take a look at our recommended dishes below


Leaving

Once you have enjoyed your delecious food, be sure to pack your dishes on a tray and leave them at the tray deposit area. Some hawkers have people walk around and collect trays but the majority don't.
