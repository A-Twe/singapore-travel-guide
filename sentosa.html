Sentosa Island

Sentosa Island

Sentosa is a sunny island in Singapore home to exciting events, themed attractions, award-winning spas, luch rainforests, golden sandy beaches and more. This popular destination was once a military fortress during WW2 which can be explored all for free! Sentosa is a fantastic day trip out or luxury place to stay for your time in Singapore.

Key information

Travel

We recommend travelling into Sentosa via the Vivo City shopping center and using the 'sky-train' or cable cars. Taking a taxi directly onto the Sentosa Island can be expensive as cars have to pay an enterance fee. Some taxi drivers will actually refuse to drive onto the island as they will incur a fee when the exit again.


Activities

Sentosa is a fairly large island and has many different areas with activities. Be sure to have something planned for when you arrive as travelling across the island to find activities can be quite a hassle. Activities also become sold out very quickly so make sure to book activites in advance. We recommend sticking to 1 activity per day to get the full experience.


Food

Sentosa have a huge variety of places to eat so don't get caught out by eating at main attractions. Eating in the theme parks or activity locations can be very expensive, so we recommend searching around the island for some better deal. *Checkout our recommendations below
